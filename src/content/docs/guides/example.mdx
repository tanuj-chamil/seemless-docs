Guides lead a user through a specific task they want to accomplish, often with a sequence of steps.
Writing a good guide requires thinking about what your users are trying to do.

## Using Mermaid Diagrams

With astro-mermaid integrated, you can easily add diagrams to your documentation using Mermaid syntax. Use the toggle to switch between the rendered diagram and the source code.

<MermaidToggle code={`graph TD;
    A[Start] --> B{Is it working?};
    B -->|Yes| C[Great!];
    B -->|No| D[Debug];
    D --> B;
    C --> E[End];`} />
